More Than a Model

Kayla Clark is a DMV-based model and creative director known for blending elegance, emotion, and intentional storytelling. Whether she's in front of the camera or shaping the vision behind it, Kayla brings a thoughtful, collaborative energy to every project.

Her work spans beauty, editorial, commercial, and conceptual shoots, and she's recognized for her ability to bring moodboards to life with precision and artistry. Kayla's creative process is rooted in collaboration, thoughtful planning, and a deep love for visual storytelling.

She believes every image should feel like emotion in motion.
