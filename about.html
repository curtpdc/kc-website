About Kayla

Kayla Clark is a professional model based in the DMV. Her work blends elegance, emotion, and intentional storytelling — bringing creative visions to life with every photoshoot.

She specializes in beauty, editorial, and commercial shoots, known for her ability to embody diverse concepts with precision and artistry. Kayla's modeling approach is rooted in collaboration, thoughtful execution, and a deep love for visual storytelling.

Every project begins with understanding the story that needs to be told. Whether working with photographers, brands, or fellow creatives, Kayla approaches each collaboration with curiosity, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

She believes every image should feel like emotion in motion — capturing not just a moment, but the feeling behind it.
