About Kayla

Kayla Clark is a model and creative director based in the DMV. Her work blends elegance, emotion, and intentional storytelling — whether she's in front of the camera or shaping the creative vision behind it.

She specializes in beauty, editorial, commercial, and conceptual shoots, and she's known for her ability to bring moodboards to life with precision and artistry. Kayla's creative process is rooted in collaboration, thoughtful planning, and a deep love for visual storytelling.

Every project begins with understanding the story that needs to be told. Whether working with photographers, brands, or fellow creatives, Kayla approaches each collaboration with curiosity, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

She believes every image should feel like emotion in motion — capturing not just a moment, but the feeling behind it.
